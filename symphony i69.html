Highlights

Symphony i69 comes with 6.09 inches HD+ IPS screen. It has a Full-View waterdrop-notch design. The back camera is of dual 13+2 MP with autofocus, LED Flash, bokeh, HDR etc. and Full HD video recording. The front camera is of 8 MP. Symphony i69 comes with 3500 mAh battery. It has 2 GB RAM, 1.6 GHz octa-core CPU and PowerVR GE8322 GPU. It is powered by a UNiSOC SC9863A (28 nm) chipset. The device comes with 16 GB internal storage and up to 128 GB expandable MicroSD slot. There is a back-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, Face Unlock etc.
