Highlights

Xiaomi 11T comes with 6.67 inches Full HD+ AMOLED screen. It has a Full-View center punch-hole design. The frontside is protected by a Gorilla Glass Victus. The back camera is of triple 108+8+5 MP with PDAF, dual-LED flash, ultrawide, telephoto macro camera, etc. and 4K video recording. The front camera is of 16 MP. 11T comes with 5000 mAh battery with 67W fast charging. It has 8 GB RAM, up to 3.0 GHz octa-core CPU and Mali-G77 MC9 GPU. It is powered by a MediaTek Dimensity 1200 5G (6 nm) chipset. The device comes in 128 or 256 GB UFS 3.1 internal storage and no Memory Card slot. There is a side-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is Dual SIM, face unlock, USB Type-C, Infrared etc.
