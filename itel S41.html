Highlights

itel has already gained a strong position in the Bangladeshi market. They usually release attractive low budget phones with fine camera quality, performance and features. itel S41 is another add to this category. You are going to have a 5″ IPS HD display, 3 GB RAM, 16 GB ROM, 8+8 MP camera, fingerprint, OTG, dual SIM and 4G supported smartphone just for BDT 8,490/=. This is an excellent deal for low-budget consumers in 2018.

The weak side is that the battery is not that powerful. It is also not a full-view design phone. RAM is 3 GB but the MediaTek chipset and1.25 GHz quad-core CPU are not that speedy. So, you won’t have the performance you may expect from a mid-range 3 GB RAM phone with a superior CPU and chipset. Still, when almost all other brands are offering 1 or 2 GB RAM phones at this price, itel has taken a very courageous step for winning the buyers.
