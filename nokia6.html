Highlights


Nokia, a brand that used to lead the mobile phone industry. Without Nokia the world of phones could have been very different today. After a few years of break, Nokia is back in the scene with a few releases in 2017. Nokia 6 has been one of the most important releases for the company.

The design of Nokia 6 is pretty interesting, elegant and almost one of a kind which is something they need at this point. The body is made with high-grade metal (aluminum) with precise finishing and refined curved edges.

Nokia 6 has some pretty cool features including powerful battery, excellent 16 MP back camera, 5.5″ large Full HD display (great view under bright sunlight and wide colour reproduction) with gorilla glass protection, octa-core CPU, android Nougat 7.0 OS (upgradable to v9.0 Pie) , 3 GB RAM, 32 GB ROM, OTG, fingerprint, Dolby sound, fast battery charging and so on. Its dual speakers and dedicated amplifier produce clear sound and deep bass. Back camera takes appreciable photos in low light which is another big plus. Snapdragon 430 chipset with Nokia’s optimized software works pretty smooth for day to day use. We recommend the 4 GB version for heavier graphics usage and multitasking.

The price of Nokia 6 is also pretty decent for all these features. The build quality is solid and the interface is well-responsive. This gadget may help Nokia get back in track again in the smartphone market.
