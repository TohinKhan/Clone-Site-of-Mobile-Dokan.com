Highlights


Great display, ultra-premium design, excellent performance, clean audio and fantastic cameras summarises Nokia 7 Plus. It is indeed a beautiful thing to look at. 2.75mm copper edges with a fine diamond cut. High quality 6000 series aluminum body, ceramic feel coating, laminated & polarized 3rd generation gorilla glass and a 6-inches full-view full HD+ display explain the Nokia 7 plus on the outside. The 12+13 MP dual camera on the back with Nokia’s classic Carl Zeiss Optics and 2x optical zoom will help to capture some fabulous shots. Then there is a 16 MP front camera for taking photos and video actions with no compromise. For advanced photographers, there is a pro camera mode to quickly adjust white balance, focus, ISO, shutter speed, and exposure. Then comes the Ozo audio with three mics for clear audio on both sides of the call. 4GB RAM, 64GB ROM, Android Oreo, Adreno 512 GPU, octa-core CPU, UHD video, OTG, fingerprint and a powerful 3800 mAh battery with up to two days of backup for frequent users. You can confidently call it a masterpiece. No, we didn’t find any disadvantages. Although, some might prefer this lovely piece to be waterproof.
