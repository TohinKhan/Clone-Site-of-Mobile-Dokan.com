Highlights

Big Battery

4000 mAh battery for the low budget consumers is among the top features of itel P33. It should provide two days backup to average users with one single charge. More frequent users may get about a day backup which is a pretty good deal in this price range.

Fingerprint Sensor

The back-mounted fingerprint sensor can be a dear thing for those who frequently unlocks their phone and still want to have a high security.

Dual Back Camera

One may argue about the necessity of a 0.8 Megapixel second camera on the back. It will help to capture a bit better details. Moreover, the dual setup looks more premium as it is trending in 2019. 5 MP front camera is of deent quality as well.

HD+ Display

Some of the phones in this budget range are offering FWVGA display resolution. The HD+ resolution means clearer and sharper view which is more pleasing and comfortable to the eyes especially while watching videos.

Reasonable Performance & Internet Browsing

We have got 3G network, Android Oreo Go, 1 GB RAM and Mali-T820 GPU. It isn’t the best performance phone out there for the cost. But itel had to make a compromise considering the features mentioned above. Still, the device can run basic internet apps like Google Chrome, Gmail, Maps, YouTube Go etc. quite smoothly because of the Go version. Everyday tasks should also be smooth enough when handled with care and patience.
