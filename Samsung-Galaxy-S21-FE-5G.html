Highlights

Samsung Galaxy S21 FE 5G comes with 6.4 inches Full HD+ Dynamic AMOLED 2X screen. It has a center punch-hole front camera design. The back camera is of triple 12+8+12 MP with PDAF, OIS, 3x optical zoom, telephoto, ultrawide etc. and up to 4K video recording. The front camera is of 32 Megapixel. Galaxy S21 FE 5G comes with 4500 mAh battery with 25W fast charging. It has 8 GB RAM, up to 2.9 GHz octa-core CPU and Mali-G78 MP14 GPU. It is powered by a 5 nm Exynos 2100 chipset. The device comes with 128 GB internal storage and no MicroSD slot. There is an optical in-display fingerprint sensor in this phone.

So, it has some fine advantages in this price range, but you must consider that a few important features are missing.
