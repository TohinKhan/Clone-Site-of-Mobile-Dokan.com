Highlights

Walton Primo X series was market shaking back in 2013 when Primo X2 came out. It was such a thriving phone for that time from a Bangladeshi brand. Primo X3 was also quite successful. But Primo X4 and X4 Pro couldn’t make a big impression in 2016 because brands like Xiaomi and Huawei were becoming strong in BD and Symphony’s Helio phones were giving some more competition as well. We didn’t see any X series phone from Walton in 2017. Now, at the end of 2018, they have released their premium line Walton Primo X5. Let us evaluate this device considering the standard of current market.

The design of this gadget is admirable with the glass back, metal frame and scratch resistant glass on the front with a Full-View structure and Full HD+ screen. But there is a full top bezel for the front camera and quite a lot of empty black space on the bottom side. More advanced design such as a notch, camera in a hole or teardrop design could be more suiting here. Strong display protection such as Gorilla Glass wasn’t used as well. So, the design and display have a few drawbacks but overall satisfactory.

The 3450 mAh battery is not too bad, but it is nothing credible as well. Same applies to both of the cameras. They are passable but nothing extraordinary to stand out in the competition. It is coming with a 6 GB RAM and Mali G71 GPU, but we couldn’t find any info about the chipset. For the most part, we can say that it will perform well like any other top mid-range smartphones around the price. Walton Primo X5 is also coming with USB Type-C, OTG, Face Unlock, Fingerprint etc. features. Overall, it is an up to the standard mid-range device with a solid performance. But the battery, camera and design couldn’t make any strong impression. More advanced features such as Gorilla Glass, waterproof body, liquid cooling, 4K video etc. are missing too. We couldn’t find any info about fast battery charging technology as well. We still consider Primo X5 as a premium mid-range gadget. But the disadvantages mentioned above are putting it slightly behind in the contest.
