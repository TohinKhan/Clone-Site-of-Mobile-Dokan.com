Highlights

itel Vision 1 comes with 6.08 inches HD+ IPS screen. It has a Full-View waterdrop notch design. The back camera is of dual 8+0.3 MP with autofocus, LED flash, low-light shots etc. and Full HD video recording. The front camera is of 5 MP. itel Vision 1 comes with 4000 mAh battery. It has 2 GB RAM, 1.6 GHz octa-core CPU and PowerVR GE8322 GPU. It is powered by a 28 nm UniSoC SC9863A chipset. The device comes with 32 GB internal storage and up to 128 GB dedicated MicroSD slot. There is a back-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, face unlock etc.
