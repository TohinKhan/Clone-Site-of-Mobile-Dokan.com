Highlights

Oppo A92 comes with 6.5 inches Full HD+ IPS LCD screen. It has a punch-hole front camera design. The back camera is of quad 48+8+2+2 MP with PDAF, ultrawide, depth sensor, dedicated macro camera, LED flash etc. and Full HD video recording. The front camera is of 16 MP. Oppo A92 comes with 5000 mAh battery with 18W fast charging. It has 8 GB RAM, up to 2.0 GHz octa-core CPU and Adreno 610 GPU. It is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 665 (11 nm) chipset. The device comes with 128 GB internal storage and MicroSD slot. There is a side-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, face unlock etc.
