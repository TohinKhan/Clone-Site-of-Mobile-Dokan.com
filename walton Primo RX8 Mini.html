Highlights

Walton Primo RX8 Mini comes with 6.3 inches Full HD+ IPS screen. It has a full-view waterdrop notch design. The back camera is of triple 12+8+5 MP with PDAF, depth sensor, LED flash, f/1.8 aperture, Sony IMX 486, sensor, 1/2.86″ large sensor, etc. features and Full HD video recording. The front camera is of 13 MP. Primo RX8 Mini comes with 3600 mAh battery with 18W Fast Charging support. But there is a 10W charger included in the box. It has 4 GB RAM, up to 2.2 GHz octa-core CPU and Adreno 512 GPU. It is powered by a 14 nm Qualcomm Snapdragon 660 chipset. The device comes with 64 GB internal storage and shared MicroSD slot. There is a back-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, face unlock, VoLTE support, USB Type-C etc.
