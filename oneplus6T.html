Highlights

OnePlus 6T comes with an on-screen fingerprint sensor or so-called “Screen Unlock”. It means, more security, quicker and more accurate unlock. It has a 6.41-inch big Full HD+ Optic AMOLED display. The display can adjust to different lighting conditions, provide clear-view and comfort for the eyes. The frames are made of metal, a 6th generation Gorilla Glass protects the display and the back side is made of durable glass. The slim designed OnePlus 6T is water and dustproof as well. It will survive under rainy or dusty conditions but be careful before submerging it into the water excessively.

16+20 MP dual back camera with so-called “nightscape” mode will help you capture excellent night shots. OIS, studio-lighting, AI scene detector, RAW Image, etc. features will help you advance in mobile photography. The front camera is 16 MP with 25mm wide-angle lens. The 4K video recordings can be edited via built-in professional video editing software. OnePlus 6T comes in 6 GB and 8 GB RAM version. Both versions are made with Qualcomm’s Snapdragon 845 chipset for super-fast performance. The GPU is Adreno 630 for supporting top-end graphic required apps. 3700 mAh battery will last more than a day with one single charge for intensive users. More impressively, the phone can be fully charged in just half an hour with the advanced fast charging technology. The phone is also offering excellent sound quality, USB Type-C earphone, OTG, etc. There is no external memory card slot, FM radio or 3.5mm jack in this gadget which can be seen as a disadvantage. But overall, a stunning premium smartphone with superb pricing.
