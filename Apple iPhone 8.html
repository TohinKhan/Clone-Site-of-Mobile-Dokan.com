Highlights

Apple is an established name in smartphone and electronics industry. Many innovative and modern features of a smartphone are originally made in Apple’s lab. Apple iPhone 8 is the 2016 version of Apple’s smartphone concept together with the iPhone 8+. So, what is different than the previous iPhone 7 and 7+?

Apple iPhone 8 is coming with a front and back glass design for the first time in an iPhone. Now you won’t feel any cheap plastic or cold metal while holding your iPhone in hand. Rather, you will feel elegant and smooth glass. Another fine improvement is its camera. Both front and back camera quality has been improved and they are arguably the best smartphone cameras of 2016. Better sensor, new color filter, and deeper pixels are some of the new adding in its camera. The True Tone technology ensures a brilliant viewing experience. The while balance of your display will adjust automatically to the environment around you to give you a clear and comfortable experience. Not to mention that the color accuracy is also tremendous in iPhone 8.

The all-new A11 Bionic chipset from Apple is up to 70& faster than the previous A10 fusion in iPhone 7. Also, the three core GPU is 25% faster than the previous generation. An added performance controller improves battery life. Wireless charging is another interesting feature for those who don’t want too much cable around them. Last but not least, there is the new iOS 11 with improved security and performance optimization.
