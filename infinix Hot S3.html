Highlights

Infinix Hot S3 is another low-mid range smartphone focused on selfie camera and smooth performance. The main focus is the 20 MP front camera with dual-LED flash that supposed to provide fine shots in low light conditions. Then there is the bokeh mode as well which is getting more popular in front camera in recent days. The display is a large 5.7-inch one. The full-view display is also offered with an HD+ resolution. The back camera is 13 MP autofocus with dual-flash. 4000 mAh battery is one of its strongest features which can provide up to 33 hours of 2G talk-time. Then there is 3 GB RAM, Android Oreo 8.0, Adreno 505 GPU and 1.4 GHz octa-core CPU with a Snapdragon 430 chipset. It should ensure lag-free performance and handle medium graphics tasks well which is an attractive deal for the pricing.

Then there is also fingerprint, OTG, face unlock, etc. features. Altogether this is a great release from the Hong-Kong based Infinix brand in this price range.
