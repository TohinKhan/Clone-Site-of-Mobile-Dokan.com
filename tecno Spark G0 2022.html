Highlights

Tecno Spark Go 2022 comes with 6.52 inches HD+ IPS LCD screen. It has a waterdrop notch design. The back camera is of dual 13 MP + AI Lens. It has PDAF, LED flash, HDR etc. features and Full HD video recording option. The front camera is of 8 MP. Tecno Spark Go 2022 comes with 5000 mAh big battery. It has 2 GB RAM, 1.8 GHz quad-core and PowerVR GE8320 GPU. It is powered by a Mediatek Helio A20 (12nm) chipset. The device comes with 32 GB internal storage and dedicated MicroSD slot. There is a back-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, face unlock, Android 11 Go Edition etc.
