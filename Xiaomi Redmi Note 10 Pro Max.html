Highlights

Xiaomi Redmi Note 10 Pro Max comes with 6.67 inches Full HD+ Super AMOLED screen. It has a center punch-hole design on the front. The display is protected by 5th generation Gorilla Glasses. The back camera is of quad 108+8+5+2 MP with PDAF, ultrawide, depth sensor, dedicated macro camera etc. and 4k Ultra HD video recording. The front camera is of 16 MP. Redmi Note 10 Pro Max comes with 5020 mAh big battery with a 33W Fast Charging solution. It has 6 or 8 GB RAM, up to 2.3 GHz octa-core CPU and Adreno 618 GPU. It is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 732G (8 nm) chipset. The phone comes with 64 or 128 GB internal storage and dedicated MicroSD slot. There is a side-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is USB type-C, Dual SIM, face unlock, Infrared etc.
