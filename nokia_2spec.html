Highlights


Nokia gave a solid comeback with their Android phones a few months back when they introduced Nokia 3, 5, and 6 smartphones. After that, the more premium Nokia 7 and 8 were released. These were quite successful as well. Now, they brought more of a budget phone named Nokia 2. But here in Bangladesh, they may face some challenges with this release.

So, why Nokia 2 can become a flop especially in Bangladesh or other Asian and south-east Asian countries? The price of Nokia 2 is almost 10K. For 10K, they are offering features like 8 GB ROM, 1 GB RAM, Snapdragon 212 chipset and HD video recording. Companies like Symphony are Giving these feature for half the price. For 10K you will comfortably get 2 GB RAM, 16 GB ROM and full HD video recording from brands like Lava, Symphony or Walton. Nokia is a great brand, but the consumers may think twice before paying this price for a phone with some relatively low-performance factors.


But on the other hand, the design is quite lovely, and the build quality is excellent. On top of that, there is 4G network support, and it comes with a 4100 mAh powerful battery with up to 1340 hours of 3G standby time. These are outstanding features, and they will get a good stand based on the positive characteristics. But we do think that the RAM, ROM, and Chipset are not worth the price. It is for you to decide if you would prioritize brand, internet speed and battery backup over total performance.
