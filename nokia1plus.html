Highlights


Durable Body & Modern Design

Nokia 1 Plus comes with a so-called 3D nano-pattern polycarbonate shell backside. It is kind of a premium plastic material that looks and feels unique. It doesn’t get scratches very easy and the grip should be quite comfortable as well. The Full-View 5.45 inches screen looks premium as well. The viewing angle of the FWVGA+ display is overall quite good and it should also be clear in bright outdoor conditions.

Nokia 4G support

Why are we mentioning “Nokia” with 4G support? It is because Nokia is well-known for its fine technolohy when it comes to network connection. You may enjoy stronger network connectivity, GPS signal, clearer call quality and sound.

Nokia Camera

Yes, here we must mention Nokia again when it comes to the camera. One can’t simply compare an 8 MP Nokia autofocus primary camera and 5 MP front camera with just any other low-budget phone. Nokia is renowned for its cameras that capture authentic natural shots with accurate colors, fine details and a good amount brightness.

Android Pie Go Performance

Nokia 1 Plus is one of the first smartphones to come with an Android Pie Go version. It is better optimized, more up to date with higher security than the previous Android Oreo Go version. The MediaTek chipset with 1 GB RAM should provide overall decent and smooth performance for everyday tasks. Apps like YouTube, Google Chrome, Google Maps, Gmail etc. should run faster as usual because of Go app versions.
