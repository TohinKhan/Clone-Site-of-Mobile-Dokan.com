Highlights

Nokia 220 (4G) comes with dual SIM. The main specialty of this phone is that it comes with 4G support. Apps like WhatsApp and Facebook are pre-installed. There is stereo FM radio in this device which is playable with 3.5mm earphone. The display is 2.4 inches. Some basic games are playable in this feature phone. There are also flashlight, loudspeaker, dedicated MicroSD slot, 1200 mAh battery, VGA back camera with flash, video recording, Bluetooth and so on.

There is no front camera on Nokia 220 (4G) which is one of its drawbacks.
