Highlights

Motorola Moto G60 comes with 6.8 inches Full HD+ screen. It has a Full-View punch-hole design. The back camera is of triple 108+8+2 MP with LED flash, PDAF, depth sensor, ultrawide camera etc. and UHD 4K video recording. The front camera is of 32 MP. Moto G60 comes with 6000 mAh battery with 20W fast charging solution. It has 6 GB RAM, up to 2.3 GHz octa-core CPU and Adreno 618 GPU. It is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 732G (8 nm) chipset. The device comes with 128 GB internal storage and shared MicroSD slot. There is a back-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, face unlock, USB Type-C etc.
