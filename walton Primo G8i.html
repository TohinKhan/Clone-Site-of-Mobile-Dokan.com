Highlights

4G Support

4G network is an essential feature for a modern smartphone user. It offers much faster internet browsing possibility thna a 3G network. Walton Primo G8i 4G is bringing this important feature at a much lower cost enabling users to be more productive in their daily life. However, the lower price 3G version of Walton Primo G8i doesn’t come with this feature.

2 GB RAM

RAM or Random Access Memory isn’t everything when it comes to performance. But it does offer some fine benefits. Apps usage, multitasking etc. will be much smoother with the help of higher amount of RAM. Light apps should run lag-free without any heating issue.

Decent BSI Camera

8 MP back and 5 MP front camera should also be pretty fine considering the price. Daylight shots with the primary camera should look natural with decent sharpness and details. The front camera works well for video calling or casual photo capturing.

Face Unlock

The face unlock feature will allow the users to unlock the phone with individual face ID when there is enough light. No worry about password or pattern to be seen by someone.

Satisfactory Battery Life

The 2250 mAh battery is coming with a battery saver option. Moreover, the FWVGA+ display will consume less power than a higher quality HD+ display. Thus, the device will provide higher scree-on-time.
