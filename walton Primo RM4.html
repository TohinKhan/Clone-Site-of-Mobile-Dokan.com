Highlights

Walton Primo RM4 comes with 6.5 inches HD+ IPS screen. It has a full-view waterdrop notch design. The back camera is of triple 13+5+0.3 MP with PDAF, depth sensor, ultrawide, LED flash, f/2.0 aperture etc. features and Full HD video recording. The front camera is of 8 MP. Primo RM4 comes with 5950 mAh big battery. It has 4 GB RAM, 1.8 GHz octa-core CPU and PowerVR GE8320 GPU. It is powered by a 12 nm MediaTek Helio A25 chipset. The device comes with 64 GB internal storage and dedicated MicroSD slot. There is a back-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone. Among other features, there is FM Radio with recording, Dual SIM, face unlock, VoLTE support, Wireless Display (Cast) etc.
