Highlights

OnePlus Nord 2 5G comes with 6.43 inches Full HD+ Fluid AMOLED screen. It has a left punch-hole front camera design. The back camera is of triple 50+8+2 MP with PDAF, OIS, ultrawide, monochrome, dual-LED flash etc. and Ultra HD video recording. The front camera is of 32 Megapixel. OnePlus Nord 2 5G comes with 4500 mAh battery with 65W fast charging. It has 8 GB RAM, up to 3.0 GHz octa-core CPU and Mali-G77 MC9 GPU. It is powered by a MediaTek Dimensity 1200 5G (6 nm) chipset. The device comes with 128 GB internal storage and no MicroSD slot. There is an optical in-display fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is Dual SIM, Face Unlock, USB Type-C etc.
