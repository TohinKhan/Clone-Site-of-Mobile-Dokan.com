Highlights

Xiaomi Redmi 9A comes with 6.53 inches HD+ screen. It has a Full-View waterdrop notch design. The back camera is of 13 MP with PDAF, LED flash, HDR etc. features and Full HD video recording option. The front camera is of 5 MP. Xiaomi Redmi 9A comes with 5000 mAh big battery with 10W fast charging. It has 2 GB RAM, 2.0 GHz octa-core CPU and PowerVR GE8320 GPU. It is powered by a MediaTek Helio G25 (12 nm) chipset. The device comes with 32 GB internal storage and dedicated MicroSD slot. There is no fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is Hybrid Dual SIM, face unlock, USB Type-C, Infrared etc.
