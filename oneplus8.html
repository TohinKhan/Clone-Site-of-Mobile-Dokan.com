Highlights

OnePlus 8 comes with 6.55 inches Full HD+ Fluid AMOLED screen. It has a punch-hole front camera design. The back camera is of triple 48+16+2 MP with PDAF, OIS, ultrawide, dedicated macro camera, dual-LED flash etc. and Ultra HD video recording. The front camera is of 16 Megapixel. OnePlus 8 comes with 4300 mAh battery with 30W fast charging. It has 8 GB RAM, up to 2.84 GHz octa-core CPU and Adreno 650 GPU. It is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 865 (7nm+) chipset. The device comes with 128 GB internal storage and no MicroSD slot. There is an optical in-display fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is Dual SIM, Face Unlock, USB Type-C etc.
