Highlights

Samsung Galaxy Note 10 Lite comes with 6.7 inches Full HD+ Super AMOLED screen. It has a punch-hole front camera design. The back camera is of triple 12+12+12 MP with PDAF, LED flash, telephoto, ultrawide, 2x optical zoom etc. and 4K video recording option. There is EIS or stabilization facility with Full HD (1080p) recording. The front camera is of 32 MP. Galaxy Note 10 Lite comes with 4500 mAh big battery and 25W fast charging. It has 6 or 8 GB RAM, up to 2.7 GHz octa-core CPU and Mali-G72 MP18 GPU. It is powered by Exynos 9810 (10 nm) chipset. The device comes with 128 internal storage and MicroSD slot option. There is an in-display fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, face unlock, Samsung DeX experience, Stylus Pen etc.
