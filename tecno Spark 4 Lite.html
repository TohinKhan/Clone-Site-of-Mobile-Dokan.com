Highlights

Tecno Spark 4 Lite comes with 7 inches HD+ IPS LCD screen. It has a waterdrop notch design with colorful gradient back. The back camera is of quad 13+2+2 MP + QVGA. It has quad-LED flash, HDR, PDAF, f/2.0 aperture etc. features and Full HD video recording option. The front camera is of 8 MP. Tecno Spark 4 Lite comes with 6000 mAh big battery and 10W fast charging facility. It has 3 GB RAM, 2 GHz quad-core CPU and PowerVR GE8320 GPU. It is powered by a Mediatek Helio P22 (12nm) chipset. The device comes with 32 GB internal storage and dedicated MicroSD slot. There is a back-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.


Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, face unlock etc.
