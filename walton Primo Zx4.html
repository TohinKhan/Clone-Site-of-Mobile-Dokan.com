Highlights

Walton Primo ZX4 comes with 6.67 inches Full HD+ LTPS IPS screen. It has a full-view punch-hole design. The back camera is of penta 64+8+5+2+2 MP main camera with PDAF, depth sensor, macro, LED flash, ultrawide, etc. features and 4K video recording. The front camera is of 32 MP. Primo ZX4 comes with 4000 mAh battery and 18W fast charging capacity battery. It has 8 GB RAM, up to 2.05 GHz octa-core CPU and Mali-G76 MC4 GPU. It is powered by a 12 nm MediaTek Helio G95 chipset. The device comes with 128 GB internal storage and shared MicroSD slot. There is a side-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, face unlock, VoLTE support, USB Type-C etc.
