Highlights

Tecno Spark 8 Pro comes with 6.8 inches Full HD+ IPS LCD screen. It has a punch-hole design with stylish back. The back camera is of triple 48 MP main camera. It has PDAF, quad-LED flash, HDR etc. features. The front camera is of 8 MP. Tecno Spark 8 Pro comes with 5000 mAh big battery and 33W fast charging facility. It has 4 or 6 GB RAM, up to 2.0 GHz octa-core CPU and Mali G52 GPU. It is powered by a Mediatek Helio G85 (12nm) chipset. The device comes with 64 GB internal storage and dedicated MicroSD slot. There is a side-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, face unlock etc.
