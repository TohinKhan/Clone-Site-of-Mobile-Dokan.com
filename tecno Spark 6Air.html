Highlights

Tecno Spark 6 Air comes with 7.0 inches HD+ IPS LCD screen. It has a waterdrop notch design with gradient back. The back camera is of triple 13 + 2 MP + AI Lens. It has PDAF, quad-LED flash, f/1.8 aperture etc. features and Full HD video recording option. The front camera is of 8 MP. Tecno Spark 6 Air comes with 6000 mAh big battery and 10W fast charging facility. It has 2 or 3 GB RAM, 1.8 GHz octa-core or quad-core CPU and PowerVR GE8320 GPU. It is powered by a Mediatek Helio A25 (12nm) chipset. The device comes with 32 or 64 GB internal storage and dedicated MicroSD slot. There is a back-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.


Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, face unlock, Android 10 Go Edition etc.
