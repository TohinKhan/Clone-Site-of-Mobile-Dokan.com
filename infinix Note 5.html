Highlights

Infinix Note 5 is one of Infinix’s unique releases. The price is under 15K in Bangladesh. With this pricing, they are targeting a huge number of low-mid range consumers.

4500 mAh large battery with so-called “Xcharge supercharge facility” can provide almost three days of backup for regular users, up to 40 hours of 2G talk-time and fast charging facility. 6-inch FHD+ infinity display will surely provide a premium viewing experience. Full HD resolution is of a high advantage as most phones around this price range is offering HD resolution. The metal print body is another premium design factor which is hardly available in this price range. 12 MP back camera with autofocus, bokeh effect, large 1.25µm pixel size and 16 MP front camera with so-called “low light AI selfie” adjusts and improves the lighting of the environment in low-light. 2 GHz octa-core CPU, Helio P23 chipset, Mali G71 graphics and 3 GB RAM ensures smooth app using, graphics tasks, browsing, multitasking and so on. The phone comes with the Android One version. This means more simplistic and lighter user interface with faster OS updates. OTG, fingerprint, dedicated MicroSD card slot, etc. are its some other features.

There is no display protection such as gorilla glass which can be seen as a disadvantage of this device.
