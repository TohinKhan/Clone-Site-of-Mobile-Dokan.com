Highlights


Nokia 3 is a budget android smartphone for everyday use. Now, within this price range, there are also a few good choices from Samsung, Huawei, Xiaomi, Symphony, Walton etc. brands. Why choose Nokia 3?

Nokia 3 has an individualistic flat design with a polycarbonate body and aluminum frame. The well-crafted design stands out pretty well. There is also corning gorilla glass protection for the display, which is quite rear in this price range. Also, the laminated and polarised HD display will provide fine view under bright daylight. Few other cool features are 4G network support, OTG, NFC, 8+8 MP autofocus camera and android Nougat OS. These features will give a premium feel at a relatively low price. 2 GB RAM, 16 GB ROM and quad-core CPU will make sure smooth performance and adequate storage capacity. Nokia has always been well-known for its great battery capacity. Nokia 3 is no different. Overall, it is a pretty handsome gadget for the price.
