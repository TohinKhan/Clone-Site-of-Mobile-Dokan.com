Highlights

Nokia 105 (2019) comes with single and dual mini-SIM options. The main specialty of this phone is that it comes with long battery life with up to 619 hours of standby time and up to 14 hours 24 minutes of talk-time. There is FM radio in this device which is playable with 3.5mm earphone. The display is 1.77 inches QQVGA quality. Some basic games are playable in this feature phone. There are also flashlight and loudspeaker.

There is no front or back camera on Nokia 105 (2019) which are some of its drawbacks.
