Highlights

Infinix Smart 3 Plus comes with a triple back camera setup at a reasonably low price tag, which is its main specialty. There is also a fast fingerprint sensor at the back. The performance of this phone should be fine for everyday usage, but heavy users may feel some lag. The design looks quite stylish altogether. It included all the necessary sensors such as E-Compass, Gyroscope and Ambient Light. The HD+ screen quality should be pleasing and the big 6.2 inches display should provide more comfort to the eyes than smaller screens.

Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, face unlock etc.
