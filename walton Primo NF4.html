Highlights

5.99-inches Big Screen

Walton Primo NF4 is offering an almost 6-inch display at a low-budget. The FWVGA+ resolution is decent considering the price and the IPS technology ensures comfortable viewing. The display is one of the biggest advantages of this device especially, for those looking for a broader viewing experience. Moreover, it is coming with stylish gradient back options.

4G Support with Android Oreo Go

4G network support enables this phone to perform faster when it comes to internet browsing. Furthermore, The Android Oreo Go operating system is offering Go version of YouTube, Gmail, Google Maps, Google Chrome etc. It means a faster operation of these apps and mobile data saving option while using them. MediaTek chipset with 1 GB RAM and quad-core CPU means smooth performance for everyday tasks and light apps usage.

Impressive Camera with EIS

The 8 MP autofocus back camera with Sony sensor can be considered impressive for the price range. Same goes to the 8 MP front camera with soft LED flash to capture better night shots. Both cameras are coming with EIS facility which means stable video shooting on the go.

Decent 3200 mAh Battery

Battery performance is one of the major concerns in low budget phones. 3200 mAh battery of Primo NF4 should provide a whole day support for average users with one single charge. It belongs to one of the better battery backups in this price range.

Fingerprint Sensor

The combination of 4G and fingerprint sensor in this price range is one of the biggest advantages of this device. Fingerprint unlock allows the user to comfortably unlock the phone anywhere with their own unique fingerprint which can’t be stolen.

OTG & Dedicated MicroSD Slot

Many users still want the OTG feature which is sometimes not available in low-budget phones like this. But we have got confirmation of OTG feature and also dedicated MicroSD slot expandable up to 64 GB. So, storage is also one of its positives.
