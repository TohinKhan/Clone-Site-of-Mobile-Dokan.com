Highlights

Samsung Galaxy A52s 5G comes with 6.5 inches Full HD+ Super AMOLED screen. It has a center punch-hole front camera design. The back camera is of Quad 64+12+5+5 with PDAF, OIS, ultrawide, depth sensor, dedicated macro camera etc. and Ultra HD video recording. The front camera is of 32 MP. Galaxy A52s 5G comes with 4500 mAh battery with 25W fast charging. It has 8 GB RAM, up to 2.3 GHz octa-core CPU and Adreno 618 GPU. It is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 720G (8 nm) chipset. The device comes with 128 GB internal storage and shared MicroSD slot. There is an in-display optical fingerprint sensor in this phone.

So, it has some fine advantages in this price range, but you must consider that a few important features are missing.
