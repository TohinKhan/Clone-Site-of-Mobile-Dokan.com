Highlights

Walton Primo F9 is one of the better 4G Android smartphones around BDT 5,000 in 2019. It has got 5 MP front and 5 MP back camera. Both supports LED flash. The performance is decent with 1 GB RAM and Android 9 Pie Go Edition. Regular apps and small games should run smoothly. The display is 5.45 inches with FWVGA+ quality, which is ok for the pricing. There is a full-view design and it also comes with a notification light. The ROM is 16 GB and then there is a dedicated MicroSD slot that allows up to 64 up external storage. It is one of the biggest benefits of this device. The 2500 mAh battery is adequate and should allow a regular user to go through the day swiftly.
