Highlights

Highlights Walton Primo GH9 comes with 6.1 inches HD+ IPS screen. It has a full-view waterdrop notch design. The back camera is of dual 13+0.3 MP with PDAF, depth sensor, LED flash, f/2.0 aperture etc and Full HD video recording. The front camera is of 5 MP. Primo GH9 comes with 3000 mAh battery. It has 2 or 3 GB RAM, 1.8 GHz quad-core CPU and PowerVR GE8300 GPU. It is powered by a MediaTek Helio A20 (12 nm) chipset. The device comes with 16 and 32 GB internal storage and dedicated MicroSD slot. There is a back-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is FM Radio with recording, Dual SIM, face unlock, VoLTE support etc.
