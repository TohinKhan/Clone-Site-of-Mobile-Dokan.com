Highlights

Symphony i97 is coming with Android 9 Pie version. It has a fine quality 13 MP back camera which supports portrait mode or bokeh effect (also called background blur or DSLR mode). The 8 MP front camera is a pretty adequate deal as well. The device is running on a powerful 1.6 GHz octa-core CPU and 2 GB RAM. It means multitasking should be very smooth. High graphics game like PUBG can be played with low settings in this smartphone.

Symphony i97 comes with a highly demanding fingerprint sensor at an attractive price. It also supports 4G. The battery is a decent 3200 mAh one. 5.7 inches HD+ IPS full-view screen is among the better ones around the price. The mysterious kind of gradient backside makes this phone more desirable. The gadget has 16 GB internal storage and an additional MicroSD card can be used to extend the memory up to 64 GB. Among other features there are face unlock, FM radio, dual Micro SIM, OTG and so on.
