Highlights

Nokia 800 Tough comes with dual Nano-SIM. The main specialty of this phone is that it comes with IP68 certified water resistance body and it is also MIL-STD-810G compliant which ensure military-grade toughness. The device runs on KaiOS and it has a dual-core processor with 4G support. So, apps and games can be installed easily. There is FM radio in this device which is playable with 3.5mm earphone. GPS, Bluetooth, WLAN etc. are included too. There is a 2 MP primary camera with LED flash and video recording option. It has an extended battery life which is up to 1032 hours of standby time. There is 4 GB internal storage and up to 32 GB dedicated MicroSD card slot.

There is no front camera in this device which is one of its drawbacks.
