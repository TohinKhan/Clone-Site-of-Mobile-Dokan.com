Highlights

itel A44 is the first 4G smartphone released by itel Bangladesh. It is also coming with 8 MP primary autofocus camera, 5 MP front camera, 5.45-inch full view IPS display, 2 GB RAM, 16 GB ROM, fingerprint sensor, eye-protection, etc. features. The biggest benefit is that it is a cheap 4G phone. But on the other hand, the downside is that the battery backup is quite low. Also, the Android version is 7.0 Nougat. But we were expecting an Oreo 8.0 version.

Hopefully, itel will release a low-budget Android Oreo Cheap 4G device soon as well.
