Highlights

Big Display, Massive Body

It is a smartphone for those who wants to go big. The 6.2″ display with 9.3 mm thickness makes Moto G7 Power a heavy device. There are a lot of users who want to feel the heavy weight of their phone with a full grip. It is a perfect gadget for them, especially, for those with a bigger hand. HD+ resolution with 270 PPI is decent when it comes to colors and sharpness. The notch design with the Motorola logo on the bottom stands out quite well. 3rd generation Gorilla Glass on the front and polymer glass back makes it more durable and premium.

3-day Battery + 15W TurboPower Fast Charging

The 5000 mAh battery is one of the main attractions of this phone. The HD+ display will consume less power than a Full HD+ display which allows it to go for up to 3 days with one single charge easily. It is one of the best battery deals for the price range in the current market. Moreover, the 15W Motorola TurboCharge is there to charge the massive battery fast. The charger is included in the box.

Motorola Camera

A single 12 MP back camera and a single 8 MP front camera may sound ordinary. But there are some special features which make the camera unique. There is Google Lens that can recognize objects and subjects like trees, buildings, animals, food and so on. Then there are features like High-res zoom that preserves more details than digital zoom for zoomed-in photos. The “cinemagraph” feature keeps one specific area of the scene in motion while freezing the other. There is also timelapse with 4x up to 32x speed. A screen flash feature is included for the front camera. 4K video recording is possible with the primary camera.

Snapdragon 632 + 4 GB RAM

Motorola made no compromise in the performance of Moto G7 Power. 14 nm Qualcomm Snapdragon 632 chipset with 4 GB RAM and Android Pie ensures smooth performance even during intensive multitasking and heavy graphics task.

Big Phone, Big Storage

It isn’t only big on the outside. The phone is coming with 64 GB ROM and dedicated MicroSD slot expandable up to 512 GB.

Water-Repellent

This Motorola smartphone can tolerate light-rain and water splashes because of its P2i nano coating.

Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, face unlock, USB Type-C etc.
