Highlights

Samsung Galaxy A01 comes with 5.7 inches HD+ screen. It has a Full-View waterdrop notch design. The back camera is of dual 13+2 MP with Autofocus, LED flash, depth sensor etc. and Full HD video recording. The front camera is of 5 MP. Galaxy A01 comes with 3000 mAh battery with no fast charging. It has 2 GB RAM, up to 1.95 GHz octa-core CPU and Adreno 505 GPU. It is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 439 (12 nm) chipset. The device comes with 16 GB internal storage and dedicated MicroSD slot. There is no fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, face unlock, Android 10 etc.
