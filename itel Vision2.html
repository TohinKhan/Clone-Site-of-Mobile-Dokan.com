Highlights

itel Vision 2 comes with 6.6 inches HD+ IPS screen. It has a Full-View waterdrop notch design. The back camera is of triple 13+2+0.3 MP with autofocus, LED flash etc. features and Full HD video recording. The front camera is of 8 MP. itel Vision 2 comes with 4000 mAh battery. It has 2 or 3 GB RAM, 1.6 GHz octa-core CPU and PowerVR IMG8322 GPU. It is powered by a UniSoC chipset. The device comes with 32 or 64 GB internal storage and up to 128 GB dedicated MicroSD slot. There is a back-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual 4G SIM, Face Unlock etc.
