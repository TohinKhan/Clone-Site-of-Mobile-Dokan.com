Highlights

Walton Primo H8 comes in three different colors. There are two different gradient blue colors and a gold color model available for purchase. The screen is a Full-View 5.45 inches HD+ IPS one which is a good deal for the price. The device has 180 grams of weight which is on the heavier side. It has a 2.5D curved glass, five finger multitouch and wireless display feature.

The back camera is of 8 Megapixel with Sony sensor, autofocus, LED flash ports etc. features. It supports Full HD video recording with EIS for stability. The front side also has an 8 MP camera with OmniVision sensor. The maximum video recording quality here is 720p or HD with stabilization as well. Both cameras and video recording quality are pretty good deals for the budget range.

One of the main specialties of Primo H8 is that it is coming with 3 GB RAM to ensure better multitasking, smoother interface and lag-free everyday usage. Even the 2 GB Version is a decent deal considering the lower price. The chipset is a MediaTek MT6739 and the CPU is 1.28 GHz quad-core. It resembles overall decent performance for a low-budget Android smartphone. The battery is 3200 mAh Li-polymer which should be sufficient for a full day of low to moderate usage with one single charge.
