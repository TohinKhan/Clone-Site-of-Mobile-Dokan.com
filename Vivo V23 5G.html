Highlights

Vivo V23 5G comes with 6.44 inches Full HD+ AMOLED screen. It has a medium notch front camera design. The back camera is of triple 64+8+2 MP with PDAF, ultrawide, OIS, macro lens, LED flash etc. and 4K video recording. The front camera is of dual 50+8 MP. Vivo V23 5G comes with 4200 mAh battery with 44W Fast Charging. It has 8 GB RAM, up to 2.5 GHz octa-core CPU and Mali-G68 MC4 GPU. It is powered by a MediaTek Dimensity 920 5G (6 nm) chipset. The device comes with 128 GB internal storage and no MicroSD slot. There is an in-display (optical) fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is Dual SIM, face unlock, USB Type-C, NFC etc.
