Highlights

Symphony Z50 comes with 6.5 inches HD+ IPS screen. It has a Full-View waterdrop-notch design. The back camera is of triple 13+2+2 MP with autofocus, LED Flash, portrait mode, night mode etc. and Full HD video recording. The front camera is of 8 MP. Symphony Z50 comes with 4000 mAh battery. It has 4 GB RAM, 2.6 GHz octa-core CPU and Mali-T880 GPU. It is powered by a MediaTek Helio P25 (16 nm) chipset. The device comes with 64 GB internal storage and MicroSD slot. There is a back-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, Face Unlock, digital wellbeing, parental control, smart controls etc.
