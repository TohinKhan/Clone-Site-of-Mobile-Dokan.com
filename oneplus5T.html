Highlights

Over the past few years, OnePlus introduced some amazing masterpieces in the global smartphone market. So, the expectation from OnePlus 5T has been pretty high. However, OnePlus again made no exception in fulfilling the expectations of the consumers. They have successfully launched another innovative, powerful, elegant and priceworthy device.

Its 16 + 20 MP dual primary camera packed with various features is close to competing with the cameras from iPhone X and Google Pixel 2 XL which costs about 2 times more. Clear, natural and colorful shots during the daytime may satisfy the user. However, Pixel 2 XL is still the clear winner for night shots and details is still quite better in both iPhone X and Pixel 2 XL. But the design of OnePlus 5T is definitely among the best smartphones in the current market. Especially in Asian countries where Big smartphones are highly valued. Its 6.01 inches display is more compact than the ones before because of the 18:9 full-view display. Together with aluminum body and AMOLED display, it is going to provide a classy holding and viewing experience.

With Snapdragon 835 chipset is smooth, fast and efficient. Lag should not occur at all even in a high graphics environment. Its fast dash charging solution will provide up to 1 day of charge in just 30 minutes! This is probably one of the (if not “the”) most amazing factors of 5T. 4K video recording, fingerprint sensor, great sound quality, 6/8 GB RAM, 64/128 GB ROM, octa-core CPU, latest Android Pie v9.0 update, powerful Adreno 540 graphics are some of its other big advantages. However, a water and dust proof body is missing which could make OnePlus 5T a complete device of its kind. But it is by all mean one of the best choices you can make for the price in the time of its release.
