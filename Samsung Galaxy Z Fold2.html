Highlights

Samsung Galaxy Z Fold2 comes with 7.6 inches (unfolded) Quad HD+ Dynamic AMOLED 2X screen. It has a foldable smartphone design. The back camera is of triple 12+12+12 MP with 4K video recording option. The front camera is of single 10 MP. Samsung Galaxy Z Fold2 comes with 4500 mAh battery and 25W fast charging. It has 12 GB RAM, up to 3.09 GHz octa-core CPU and Adreno 650 GPU. It is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 865+ (7 nm+) chipset. The device comes with 256 or 512 GB internal storage and no additional MicroSD slot. There is a side-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is e SIM, OTG, Bixby, Samsung DeX, face unlock, AKG tuned sound, 5G support etc. There is no FM Radio and 3.5mm Jack in this device.
