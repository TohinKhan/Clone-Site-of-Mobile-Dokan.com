Highlights

You may ask yourself this question, for the price of Samsung Galaxy Note 10+, one can buy a fine quality Laptop and a premium level smartphone. Wouldn’t that be a better choice altogether? The answer is probably yes, as far as any regular user is concerned. However, Galaxy Note 10+ comes with 12 GB RAM, 7 nm ultra fast processor, Bluetooth enabled stylus, 512 GB built-in storage, up to 1 TB MicroSD option, fabulous camera, 45W super fast charging, Wireless Screen Mirroring & HDMI, Samsung DeX Experience and a 6.8 inches Quad HD+ Dynamic AMOLED screen. This is not just a smartphone. It is a powerhouse with extreme versatility. Take Samsung DeX for example. You can connect this device to a monitor and it becomes a super-powerful yet mini CPU with a touchscreen and Stylus. Add a Bluetooth keyboard and mouse to get comfortable with your new generation desktop concept. On top of that, you can use Stylus for art projects, presentations, or in the office. Would you rather go for entertainment stuff? Then simply connect it to a smart TV via the Wireless Mirroring option and play YouTube or Netflix on your TV or play PUBG in a big screen. We are talking about 256 GB or even 512 GB of internal storage in a device that fits in hand. Add 1 TB MicroSD to that. So, it can become a device with 1.5TB storage. You can carry all that data, media etc. in your pocket and play or view them without any network or internet connection anywhere in the world. It is not just 1.5TB storage in a smartphone. It is how much it widens up the world in a tiny space and shows you life is all about making the best out of it. The fineness of this device is a true inspiration in the field of engineering. Simple and minimal yet broad and super powerful. We have similar cameras like the Galaxy S10 and the S10+. The back camera is one of the best to get in the market of 2019. But it doesn’t flip like the Galaxy A80 so, there is a 10 MP front camera which is surprisingly good as well. However, it is still just a single front camera and in no way comparable to the Quad back camera. The front and back sides are protected by Gorilla Glasses and the frames are made of premium stainless steel. The device is IP68 certified waterproof. There is an in-display ultrasonic fingerprint sensor with few other strong security features mentioned above in the specs table. This gadget can last for a decade in the hand of a fine user, but the usual warranty is for two years so, we will stick with that for the general audience.
