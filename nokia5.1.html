Highlights


Those who are looking for a durable high-quality metal body mid-range smartphone, Nokia 5.1 can be an ideal choice. 6000 series aluminum body will help increase the overall durability of the phone. Third-Generation gorilla glass will protect the 5.5 inches Full HD+ IPS front display. This device comes in two different versions. One has 2 GB RAM and 16 GB ROM and the other one has 3 GB RAM and 32 GB ROM. We are talking here about the 3 GB RAM version. Performance wise, it is also comparable with any other high-quality mid-range phone. You will get Android One as a big bonus. Android One ensures two years of free OS and security updates. So, you will get the next two Android versions in this device. You also have the option to save unlimited photos in Google Photos cloud storage. So, the internal storage won’t run out fast if you capture a lot of pictures and want to save them in your device.


Camera Quality and battery performance are quite convenient as well. Plus, if you appreciate the fine tuning and reputation of Nokia phones, this can be the device to pick without any hesitation.
