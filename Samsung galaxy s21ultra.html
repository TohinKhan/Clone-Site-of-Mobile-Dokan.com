Highlights

Samsung Galaxy S21 Ultra comes with 7.6 inches (unfolded) Full HD+ high-quality Dynamic AMOLED 2X screen. It has a foldable smartphone design. The back camera is of triple 12+12+12 MP with 4K video recording option. The front camera is of 4 MP with an extra 10 MP cover display camera. Galaxy Z Fold3 5G comes with 4400 mAh battery. It has 12 GB RAM, up to 2.84 GHz octa-core CPU and Adreno 660 GPU. It is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 888 5G (5 nm) chipset. The device comes with 256 or 512 GB internal storage and no MicroSD slot. There is a side-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is eSIM, Samsung Pay, fast wireless charging, 6.2″ cover display, waterproof body, advanced audio quality, 5G support and so on. There is no FM Radio and 3.5mm Jack in this device.
