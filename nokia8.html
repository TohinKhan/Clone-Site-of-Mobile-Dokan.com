Highlights


When it comes to high-end smartphones in 2018, one thing is quite clear. You need to bring your innovative features to stay ahead of the game. As well as present a unique, individual combination of features and elegant design to attract new consumers. Nokia gave a comeback with Android smartphones last year. Let’s see what they can do with Nokia 8 in 2018.

Nokia 8 has the individualistic and identical Nokia feel in its design. But it is missing the trendy full view display. It can be a turn off for many. It also comes with the 5.3-inch display which will be considered somewhat small. Especially, in the Asian market, high-end phones with 5.5 up to 6-inch display are more popular. So, the display is an issue and probably the only hard critical issue for big markets like China or India.

Nokia has always been famous for its cameras. Nokia 8 is no different. 13 MP back camera is packed with high-quality features including their classic Carl Zeiss optics deliver excellent shots. Innovative features like dual-sight mode will allow you to use the front and back camera at the same time and capture both sides in one single frame. The front camera is pretty good too and supports ultra HD video. Its display has a 5th generation gorilla glass protection and the QHD quality will provide a clear, bright and crisp view. The body is made of solid aluminum 6000 series which is a highly durable material. Adreno 540 GPU is excellent for gaming together with its ultra-fast RAM, everything should run smoothly even in intensive multitasking or while playing the latest HD games. 4G support will let you browse faster.

Nokia focused quite finely in the audio quality of their 8 version. The Nokia OZO audio improves the sound quality while taking a video. With Nokia 8 you can start live streaming on Facebook or YouTube with one single touch. Overall, it is a great smartphone to start 2018 and is worth its price. But if you want to enjoy the comfort of big display, this is most probably not the device you are looking for.
