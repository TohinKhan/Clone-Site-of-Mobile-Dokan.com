Highlights

Samsung Galaxy Z Flip3 5G comes with 6.7 inches (unfolded) Full HD+ high-quality Dynamic AMOLED 2X screen. It has a foldable smartphone design. The back camera is of dual 12+12 MP with 4K video recording option. The front camera is of 10 MP. Galaxy Z Flip3 5G comes with 3300 mAh battery. It has 8 GB RAM, up to 2.84 GHz octa-core CPU and Adreno 660 GPU. It is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 888 5G (5 nm) chipset. The device comes with 128 or 256 GB internal storage and no MicroSD slot. There is a side-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is eSIM, Samsung Pay, fast wireless charging, 1.9″ cover display, waterproof body, advanced audio quality, 5G support and so on. There is no FM Radio and 3.5mm Jack in this device.
