Highlights

Impressive Design

The gradient back side of Infinix S4 looks pretty elegant. On top of that, it also glitters which makes the outlook somewhat unique. The body is made of plastic though.

Stunning Camera

Camera is the main attraction of Infinix S4. It is one of the very phones to give a triple back camera setup at this price range. Yes, it has the ultra-wide mode and yes, there is the depth sensor to capture better details. The edge detection of the portrait mode works pretty accurately too. Overall, there should be a lot of light, details and natural colors, especially in bright daylight conditions. We were quite amazed by the photo samples. 32 MP front camera is also a great deal keeping the cost of the phone in mind.

Strong Battery

4000 mAh battery with 10W fast charging makes Infinix S4 an up to the mark smartphone when it comes to battery backup.

Stable Performance

2 GHz octa-core CPU supported by 3 GB RAM and MediaTek’s Helio P22 chipset is a good hardware combination around this price to ensure smooth multitasking and regular usage. Higher graphics apps may run just fine with low graphics settings.

Fingerprint Sensor

Fingerprint unlock option is a must-have feature for many users with this budget. Infinix kept that in mind by adding a back-mounted fingerprint sensor which should work fast and accurate.

Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, face unlock etc.
