Highlights

Tecno Camon 16 Premier comes with 6.9 inches Full HD+ IPS LCD screen. It has a left double punch-hole front camera design. The back camera is of quad 64+8+2+2 MP with PDAF, penta-LED flash, dedicated video camera, ultrawide, depth sensor etc. features and 4K video recording option. The front camera is of dual 48+8 MP. Tecno Camon 16 Premier comes with 4500 mAh battery and 33W fast charging facility. It has 8 GB RAM, 2.05 GHz octa-core CPU and Mali-G76 MC4 GPU. It is powered by a Mediatek Helio G90T (12nm) chipset. The device comes with 128 GB internal storage and dedicated MicroSD slot. There is a side-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is FM Radio, Dual SIM, face unlock etc.
