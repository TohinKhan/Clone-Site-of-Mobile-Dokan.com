Highlights

Symphony V141 is a low-budget 3G smartphone running on Android 8.1 Oreo Go version. It has decent quality front and back cameras, satisfactory performance for day to day usage, satisfactory battery backup, dual SIM, decent front and back cameras etc. features. It is altogether a good deal considering the price. The phone doesn’t come with OTG support and fingerprint sensor which can be seen as drawbacks. There is also no Full HD video recording option. The FWVGA+ screen helps to get longer battery backup, but quality wise, HD+ screen would have been a much more appreciated feature.

So, it has some fine advantages in this price range, but you must consider that a few important features are missing.
