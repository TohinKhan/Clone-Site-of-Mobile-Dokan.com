Highlights

Nokia 8110 (4G) is a one of a kind phone. You can’t say that it is a traditional feature phone if you look at the features. On the other hand, it is also not a usual kind of smartphone. Anyway, first of all, the sliding cover is an absolute class. Slide open to pick up calls and slide back to after finishing the call. The curved shape makes it more elegant as well. But the body is made of plastic (Polycarbonate shell). A metal body could shake the market, we think. The battery has a standby time of up to 25 days which you would expect from a pricey modern Nokia feature phone. Now, what makes Nokia 8110 (4G) is its 4G connectivity. It means you can use your internet data packs with this phone to make calls or use Facebook etc. The WAP browser is there to search on Google as well. There is a smart KaiOS with dual-core CPU, 512 MB RAM and 4 GB internal storage. Moreover, there is a Snapdragon 205 chipset from Qualcomm and Adreno 304 GPU. These specs make this device kind of a hybrid. There is just 2 MP back camera with LED flash, but no front camera and the display is just 2.4″.

There is no front or back camera on Nokia 105 (2019) which are some of its drawbacks.
