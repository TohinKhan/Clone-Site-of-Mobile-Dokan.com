Highlights

No doubt, the best thing about Walton Primo EF8 4G is its price. There are not too many 4G smartphones on the market that comes under 5,000 Tk. This gadget is specially made for those who want to have a fast internet browsing experience at the lowest possible budget. It should fulfill this purpose well.

Of course one shouldn’t expect many other high-quality features around this price range. FWVGA+ display, 5 MP primary and 5 MP front camera, 2050 mAh battery, 1 GB RAM, 8 GB internal storage puts it more on the average side. It is the Android 8.1 Go Edition operating system and 4G network support that makes the phone an exceptional choice for very low-budget consumers. Overall, the phone should function well. The cameras can capture decent daylight shots and both cameras are coming with LED flash for better night shots which is a good thing as well. Performance-wise, it is very entry-level, but it can handle regular everyday tasks if used with some patience. The battery may provide an average one-day backup to regular users.
