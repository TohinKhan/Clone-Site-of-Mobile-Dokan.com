Highlights

OnePlus Nord N10 5G comes with 6.52 inches HD+ IPS LCD screen. It has a punch-hole front camera design. The back camera is of triple 13+2+2 MP with PDAF, dedicated macro camera, depth sensor, LED flash etc. and Full HD video recording. The front camera is of 8 Megapixel. OnePlus Nord N10 5G comes with 5000 mAh battery with 18W fast charging. It has 4 GB RAM, up to 1.8 GHz octa-core CPU and Adreno 610 GPU. It is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 460 (11 nm) chipset. The device comes with 64 GB internal storage and MicroSD slot. There is a back-mounted fingerprint sensor in this phone.

Among other features, there is Dual SIM, Face Unlock, USB Type-C etc.
